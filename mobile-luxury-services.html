All Our Services Are Mobile (Except One)

What About Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic Coating is Different: Ceramic coating takes a long time and needs a super clean environment to work right. So for this service, we pick up your car, do the work at our shop, and bring it back to you. It's the only service that's not done at your location, but we make it easy with free pickup and delivery.

Everything Else is Mobile: All our other services - full detail, mobile car detailing, interior detailing, exterior detailing, paint correction, window tinting, RV detailing, boat detailing, fleet detailing - we do it all at your place with professional equipment and great results.

Mobile Luxury Service Questions

What mobile services do you offer?

We bring all our services to you: interior detailing, exterior detailing, paint correction, RV detailing, boat detailing, and window tinting. Only ceramic coating requires pickup/dropoff.

Do you bring your own water and power?

Yes! Our mobile units have everything - water tanks, generators, and all professional equipment. We can work anywhere you have parking space.

How long does mobile detailing take?

It depends on what you need. Basic services take 1-2 hours, full details take 3-4 hours. We'll tell you exactly how long when you book.

What if it's raining or snowing?

We can work in your garage or any covered area. If you don't have covered space, we can reschedule for better weather. We're flexible.

Do you charge extra for mobile service?

Nope! Mobile service is included in all our prices. We don't charge extra for coming to you - that's just how we do business.

What areas do you serve for mobile detailing?

We serve all of Niagara County including Lewiston, Clarence, East Amherst, Williamsville, Niagara Falls, and surrounding areas.

Can you do multiple cars at once?

Absolutely! If you have multiple cars, we can detail them all at the same time. It's actually more efficient and can save you money.

Why isn't ceramic coating mobile?

Ceramic coating needs a super clean, dust-free environment and takes 12+ hours to cure properly. That's why we pick up your car, do it right at our shop, and deliver it back to you.
