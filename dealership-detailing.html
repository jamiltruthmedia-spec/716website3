Dealership Detailing FAQ

Do you come to our dealership lot?

Yes. We bring everything to your lot - no need to transport cars. We work around your sales schedule and clean cars where customers see them.

How fast can you prep a car for delivery?

We offer same-day service for urgent deliveries. Most PDI work can be completed within 2-4 hours depending on the car's condition.

Do you work with high-volume dealers?

Yes. We provide monthly contracts, bulk rates, priority scheduling, and dedicated service teams for dealers with large inventories.

Can you handle shipping film removal?

Absolutely. We specialize in shipping film removal, rail dust removal, and transport residue cleaning without damaging the car's finish.

What about weather damage from sitting on lots?

We fix water spots, sun fading, minor hail damage, tree sap, and other weather-related issues that happen when cars sit outside.

Do you offer seasonal protection for lot cars?

Yes. We provide winter protection, spring refresh detailing, and summer UV protection to keep your inventory looking great year-round.
