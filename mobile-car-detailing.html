Mobile Detailing FAQ

Do you bring your own water and power?

Yes, our mobile units are completely self-contained with water tanks, generators, and all necessary equipment. We can work anywhere you have space for our vehicle, including locations without water or electrical access.

How long does mobile detailing take?

Service time varies by package: Express (1-2 hours), Premium (2-3 hours), and Luxury (3-4 hours). We'll provide an accurate time estimate when you book based on your vehicle and selected services.

What if it's raining or snowing?

We can work in light rain and have equipment for various weather conditions. For severe weather, we can reschedule or work in your garage if available. We'll always communicate about weather-related scheduling.

Do you detail boats and RVs on-site?

Yes! Our mobile service extends to boats, RVs, and other recreational vehicles. We have specialized equipment and products for marine and RV detailing. Contact us for custom quotes on larger vehicles.
