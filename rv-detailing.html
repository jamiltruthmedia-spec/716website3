RV Detailing FAQ

How often should I have my RV professionally detailed?

We recommend professional RV detailing twice per year - spring preparation before camping season and fall cleaning before winter storage. Heavy users may benefit from mid-season maintenance.

Do you provide mobile RV detailing at storage facilities?

Yes, 716 Details offers mobile RV detailing at storage facilities, campgrounds, and your home throughout Lewiston, Clarence, East Amherst, and all of Niagara County.

Can you detail all types and sizes of RVs?

Absolutely. We detail all recreational vehicles including Class A, B, and C motorhomes, travel trailers, fifth wheels, pop-up campers, and toy haulers of all sizes.

What's included in winterization detailing?

Our winter prep includes thorough cleaning, protective wax application, awning and slide-out care, interior sanitization, and seal conditioning to prevent damage during storage.

Do you clean RV roofs and awnings?

Yes, we safely clean RV roofs (when accessible) and provide thorough awning cleaning and treatment to prevent mold, mildew, and UV damage.

How long does RV detailing take?

Depending on RV size and condition, detailing typically takes 4-8 hours for a complete service. Large Class A motorhomes may require a full day for comprehensive detailing.
