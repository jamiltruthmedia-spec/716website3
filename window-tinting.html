Window Tinting FAQ

What are New York's window tint laws?

In NY, front side windows must allow more than 70% of light through. Back side windows and rear windows can be any darkness. We ensure all installations comply with state regulations.

How long does window tinting take?

Most vehicles take 2-4 hours depending on the number of windows and film type. We'll provide an accurate time estimate when you schedule your appointment.

What's the difference between ceramic and regular tint?

Ceramic tint offers superior heat rejection, won't interfere with electronics, and won't fade or turn purple over time. It's our most popular option for luxury vehicles.

Can you remove old tint and install new film?

Yes, we specialize in tint removal and replacement. We'll safely remove old, damaged tint and install new premium film with proper preparation.

Do you offer warranties on window tinting?

Yes, we offer lifetime warranties on our premium ceramic films covering fading, bubbling, peeling, and adhesion failure for complete peace of mind.

Will window tint affect my GPS or phone signal?

Our ceramic films won't interfere with GPS, radio, or cell phone signals. Only metallic tints cause signal interference, which we don't recommend.
