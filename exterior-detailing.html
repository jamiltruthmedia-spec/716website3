Exterior Detailing FAQ

How often should I get my car's exterior detailed?

We recommend exterior detailing every 3-4 months to maintain paint protection and appearance. Vehicles exposed to harsh conditions may benefit from more frequent service.

Do you offer mobile exterior detailing in Clarence and East Amherst?

Yes ‚Äî 716 Details offers mobile exterior car detailing across Clarence, East Amherst, Williamsville, Lewiston, and the entire Niagara County region.

Can you remove scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation?

Absolutely. Our paint correction process can remove most scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation to restore your paint's original clarity and gloss.

What's the difference between wax and paint sealant?

Wax provides a warm, deep gloss but lasts 2-3 months. Paint sealants offer longer protection (6-12 months) with excellent durability and UV resistance.

How long does exterior detailing take?

A complete exterior detail typically takes 3-6 hours depending on the vehicle size, condition, and level of paint correction needed.

Do you work on luxury and exotic vehicles?

Yes, we specialize in luxury and exotic vehicles. Our technicians are trained to work with all paint types, including delicate finishes and specialty coatings.
