Marine Detailing FAQ

How often should I have my boat professionally detailed?

We recommend professional marine detailing twice per season - once in spring for launch preparation and once in fall before winter storage. Boats in regular use may benefit from mid-season maintenance detailing.

Do you provide mobile boat detailing at Lake Ontario marinas?

Yes, 716 Details offers mobile marine detailing services at marinas throughout Lewiston, Youngstown, Wilson, and the entire Lake Ontario region. We bring all equipment and water to your slip.

Can you restore heavily oxidized gel coat?

Absolutely. Our gel coat restoration process can bring back severely oxidized, chalky fiberglass to like-new condition using progressive compound and polish techniques.

What's included in winterization detailing?

Our winter prep includes thorough cleaning, gel coat protection, interior sanitization, metal protection, and application of long-term protectants to prevent damage during storage.

Do you work on all types of boats?

Yes, we detail all vessel types including sailboats, powerboats, fishing boats, pontoons, jet skis, and yachts. Our techniques adapt to fiberglass, aluminum, and other marine materials.

How long does boat detailing take?

Depending on boat size and condition, marine detailing typically takes 4-8 hours for a complete service. We'll provide accurate time estimates based on your specific vessel.
