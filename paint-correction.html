Paint Correction FAQ

How long does paint correction take?

Paint correction timing depends on the vehicle size and correction level needed. Stage 1 typically takes 4-6 hours, Stage 2 takes 6-8 hours, and Stage 3 can take 8-12 hours for complete restoration.

Will paint correction remove all scratches?

Paint correction can remove scratches that haven't penetrated through the clear coat. Deep scratches that reach the paint or primer may require touch-up work before correction. We'll assess your vehicle and explain what's achievable.

How often should I get paint correction?

Most vehicles benefit from paint correction every 2-3 years, depending on maintenance and environmental exposure. Proper wash techniques and protection can extend this timeline significantly.

Do you offer paint correction for all vehicle types?

Yes, we provide paint correction for cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, boats, and RVs. We adjust our techniques and products based on the specific paint system and vehicle type.
