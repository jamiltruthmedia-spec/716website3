    Common Paint Issues We Solve

    Swirl Marks & Spider Webbing

    Eliminate the fine circular scratches created by improper washing techniques, automatic car washes, and poor-quality microfiber towels that make your paint look dull and hazy.

    Oxidation & Chalking

    Restore paint that has become faded, chalky, or milky due to UV exposure and environmental damage, bringing back the original color depth and vibrancy.

    Key Scratches & Scuff Marks

    Professional removal of intentional and accidental scratches, including shopping cart damage, key marks, and contact scratches from tight parking situations.

    Water Spot Etching

    Remove mineral deposits and etching caused by hard water, sprinkler systems, and acid rain that create permanent spots and rings on your paint surface.

    Buffer Trails & Holograms

    Correct improper polishing work that has left buffer marks, holograms, and pig-tailing patterns that are visible under certain lighting conditions.

    Paint Transfer & Scuffs

    Remove paint transfer from other vehicles, rubber marks from tires, and scuff marks that have adhered to your vehicle's clear coat surface.

    Paint Correction FAQ

    How long does paint correction take?

    Paint correction timing depends on the vehicle size and correction level needed. Stage 1 typically takes 4-6 hours, Stage 2 takes 6-8 hours, and Stage 3 can take 8-12 hours for complete restoration.

    Will paint correction remove all scratches?

    Paint correction can remove scratches that haven't penetrated through the clear coat. Deep scratches that reach the paint or primer may require touch-up work before correction. We'll assess your vehicle and explain what's achievable.

    How often should I get paint correction?

    Most vehicles benefit from paint correction every 2-3 years, depending on maintenance and environmental exposure. Proper wash techniques and protection can extend this timeline significantly.

    Do you offer paint correction for all vehicle types?

    Yes, we provide paint correction for cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, boats, and RVs. We adjust our techniques and products based on the specific paint system and vehicle type.
