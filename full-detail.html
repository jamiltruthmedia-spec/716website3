Full Detail Questions

What's included in a full detail?

Full detail includes complete exterior wash and wax, interior deep cleaning, dashboard care, window cleaning, tire shine, and paint protection. Everything to make your car look brand new.

How long does full detailing take?

Full detail takes 3-4 hours depending on your car's condition. We take our time to make sure everything is perfect.

Is full detail worth the money?

Yes! Full detail protects your car's value, makes it look amazing, and keeps it cleaner longer. It's like getting a brand new car.

Can you do full detail at my location?

Absolutely! We bring all our equipment to you. Full detail is available as mobile service throughout Niagara County.

How often should I get full detail?

Most people get full detail 2-4 times per year. More if you really love your car looking perfect, less if you just want to maintain it.

What's the difference between packages?

Standard is basic cleaning, Premium adds paint protection and leather care, Luxury includes paint correction and engine cleaning. We'll help you choose what's best.

Do you detail really dirty cars?

Yes! We love transforming really dirty cars - it's the most satisfying work we do. No car is too dirty for us to make look amazing.

Can I stay and watch?

Of course! Many customers enjoy watching the transformation. We're proud of our work and happy to explain what we're doing.
